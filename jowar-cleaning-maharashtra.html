Jowar Cleaning & Processing

At Rampushpa Agro Processing, we offer specialized Jowar (Sorghum) processing services designed for the agricultural wholesale industry. Our state-of-the-art machinery ensures high-efficiency Cleaning by removing dust, stones, and foreign particles. We implement precise Sorting and Grading processes to segregate grains by size and color, ensuring uniform quality.

Our comprehensive Quality Control measures guarantee that every batch meets stringent industry standards. Finally, our professional Packaging solutions preserve the grain's freshness, making it ready for distribution across domestic and international markets.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Jowar cleaning?

Jowar cleaning is an industrial process that removes impurities such as dust, stones, chaff, and damaged seeds from raw sorghum to ensure it meets food-grade and commercial standards.

Do you process bulk quantities?

Yes, we are equipped with high-capacity processing infrastructure to comfortably manage and deliver bulk wholesale orders for traders and commercial clients.

Do you provide packaging?

Absolutely. We provide premium packaging services using high-quality woven bags designed for durability during transportation and optimal storage.

Where is the facility located?

Our modern grain cleaning and processing units are strategically located in Beed, Maharashtra, serving clients across the region.
