Advanced Bulk Wheat Processing

For traders and millers, the purity and size consistency of wheat directly impacts profitability. Our Wheat Cleaning facility is designed to process massive volumes while maintaining exacting standards. We utilize advanced destoners to remove heavy impurities and aspiration channels for lighter dust and chaff.

Furthermore, our sophisticated Color Sorting technology identifies and ejects infected, discolored, or foreign seeds from the batch. Once cleaned, the wheat undergoes accurate Grading to separate grains based on density and size, ensuring you receive a premium product ready for milling or wholesale packaging.

The Wheat Grading Process

State-of-the-art machinery for optimal grain quality.

1

Pre-Cleaning

Screening out oversized stalks, straw, and large field debris.

2

Destoning & Aspiration

Separation of stones, clods, and dust through gravity and airflow.

3

Color Sorting

Optical sorting to remove damaged, black, or discolored grains.

4

Size Separation

Classifying wheat into uniform categories based on kernel size.

5

Weighing & Packing

Accurate bagging of the finished product for distribution.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can you remove Ergot or discolored wheat?

Yes, our advanced optical color sorters are specifically calibrated to identify and reject discolored, diseased, or foreign grains like Ergot from the healthy wheat.

What is your processing capacity for wheat?

We run high-capacity lines that can handle tens of tonnes daily, making us an ideal partner for large-scale agricultural traders and flour mills.

Do you grade wheat by size?

Absolutely. We separate shriveled or broken kernels from full, plump wheat grains to provide uniform batches that command higher market prices.

Can you pack the processed wheat under our brand name?

While we provide standard bulk packaging, we can discuss custom or white-label packaging solutions based on the scale of your contract.
