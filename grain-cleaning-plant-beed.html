Rampushpa Agro Processing Infrastructure

Located in the heart of Maharashtra, the Rampushpa Agro Grain Cleaning Plant in Beed is a beacon of agricultural modernization. Our facility spans a massive area equipped with the latest generation of destoners, gravity separators, and optical color sorters to manage diverse bulk commodities.

We provide full-scale Contract Processing for traders, farmers, and wholesale buyers across the state. Our infrastructure ensures continuous, high-efficiency output, drastically reducing turnaround times while maintaining uncompromised quality and food safety standards from raw intake to final dispatch.

Frequently Asked Questions

Where exactly is the processing plant located?

Our main agricultural processing infrastructure is located in Beed, Maharashtra, strategically positioned to serve central and southern agricultural regions.

Do you offer third-party / job-work processing?

Yes, we offer comprehensive job-work and contract processing services for traders, farmers, and wholesale distributors looking to clean and package their own inventory.

Can I visit the plant before signing a contract?

Absolutely. We encourage prospective partners to schedule a visit to our facility in Beed to witness our machinery and quality standards firsthand.

What types of grains do you process?

Our multi-purpose lines are equipped to process Jowar, Bajra, Wheat, Chana, and various other pulses and cereals.

How do you handle the logistics of bulk processing?

We have spacious intake yards, efficient unloading systems, and large-scale warehousing to smoothly manage multiple truckloads of incoming and outgoing goods daily.
