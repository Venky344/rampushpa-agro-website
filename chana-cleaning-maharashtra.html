Wholesale Chana (Chickpea) Processing

Chana (Bengal Gram) requires a precise cleaning approach to eliminate field soil, stones, and hollow pods. Our comprehensive Chana Cleaning service removes all unhygienic elements, delivering a product that meets strict food-grade and commercial standards.

We specifically focus on Sorting out split grains (dal) and damaged seeds from the whole chana, ensuring your batch consists purely of intact chickpeas. Our machinery further enables precise Grading based on size and weight, allowing us to package premium quality grain for large-scale distribution.

Our Chana Processing Lifecycle

Delivering spotless chickpeas through automated perfection.

1

Pod & Debris Screening

Initial sifting removes unthreshed pods, leaves, and large dirt lumps.

2

Gravity Destoning

Separation of heavy stones and soil particles using vibration and air.

3

Split Grain Separation

Removing broken chana (dal) to maintain whole-grain consistency.

4

Defect Sorting

Isolating insect-damaged, shriveled, or immature seeds.

5

Automated Packaging

Weighing and bagging in robust sacks ready for transport.

Frequently Asked Questions

Does your process separate Chana Dal from Whole Chana?

Yes, one of our key processing stages isolates split chickpeas (dal) from the whole grains, ensuring you get a pure, intact batch of whole chana.

Can you handle raw chana directly from the fields?

Yes. Our pre-cleaning machinery is designed to take raw agricultural produce and systematically remove field debris, soil lumps, and stalks before the fine cleaning begins.

Do you process other pulses and grams?

Yes, while chana is a primary focus, our versatile sorting and cleaning lines can be calibrated to process various other pulses and lentils.

Are you equipped to handle export-quality processing?

Absolutely. Our stringent grading and color-sorting protocols ensure the chana meets international export standards for purity and uniformity.
