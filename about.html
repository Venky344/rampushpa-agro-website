Our Story

Built on a foundation of integrity, vision, and quality since 2017.

Our History & Mission

Empowering Growth Through Quality

Established in 2017 and located in Beed, Maharashtra, Rampushpa Agro Processing has grown to become a cornerstone in the regional agricultural supply chain. Our mission is to deliver exceptional value through sustainable and innovative processing practices.

We operate a state-of-the-art Grain Cleaning and Processing facility. With our advanced infrastructure, we ensure premium quality across our entire product range—providing perfectly cleaned and sorted grains, pulses, and wheat.

Our Mission

To empower farmers through fair practices and deliver uncompromised quality grains to the market, ensuring food safety and excellence at every step.

Our Vision

To be India's most trusted name in agro-processing, known for our technological advancements, ethical sourcing, and superior end-products.

Key Strengths

Advanced Sortex Technology, Experienced Workforce, Strategic Location, Uncompromising Quality Control.
